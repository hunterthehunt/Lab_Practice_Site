Our Story

Founded by audiophiles for audiophiles, Waxxed on Waxx is more than just a retail store. We are a collection of music lovers dedicated to preserving the rich, warm heritage of analog sound processing.

Our Club History

What started in 2024 as a small gathering of four collectors swapping classic pressings in a basement garage quickly turned into an obsession. We realized that local collectors lacked a community space to safely share, verify, and trade records without steep marketplace processing fees.

By late 2025, our circle grew to over two hundred local collectors, sparking the official formation of the Waxxed on Waxx curated membership box service and interactive digital trading hub.
